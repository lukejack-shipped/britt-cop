Attendee check-in page

Attendees scan a QR code or follow a link to check in, rate the class, and see upcoming sessions. Requires name + email — automatically builds the contact database.

A practical plan for building and maintaining this database from all available sources.

Gmail — Google Contacts export Best source Do first

Everyone Britt has ever emailed is in Google Contacts. Fastest way to seed the database with real names and emails.

1.Go to contacts.google.com → Export → Google CSV
2.Open in Excel/Sheets, delete noise (newsletters, randoms)
3.Import cleaned file into this CRM using the CSV import button
~30 min one-time · Can repeat any time
Instagram — data export + manual enrichment Partial

IG gives usernames only — no emails or real names. Useful for identifying engaged followers, but requires manual follow-up. API shut down in late 2024.

1.Settings → Your activity → Download your information (JSON)
2.Scan messages/inbox/ for anyone who DM'd about training or events
3.Add as Leads — reply asking for their email to capture real contact info
Export takes up to 48 hrs · Usernames only · No API access
iMessage & WhatsApp — historical recovery Manual
iMessage:Use iMazing (~$45) on Mac to export conversation history
WhatsApp:Open each chat → three dots → Export chat → email to self
One-time recovery effort · No ongoing API for either
The email-first habit — most important long-term
→IG bio: "DM me your email for my free training guide" — converts followers to owned contacts
→Trap Yoga: Collect emails at registration — this is a goldmine of superfans
→Corporate: Always get the direct email of every stakeholder in a deal, not just the main contact
→Every inquiry: Log immediately — Britt from phone, assistant from desktop
